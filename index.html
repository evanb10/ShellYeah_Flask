How It Works

Step 1: Find Your Leagues
Enter your Sleeper username and the current season (e.g., 2025) to find your leagues. The app will look for leagues from the previous season to determine standings.

Step 2: Select Your League
Choose the league you want to run a lottery for. The app will automatically fetch the previous season's standings to identify lottery-eligible teams.

Step 3: Confirm Teams & Odds
Review the teams and their lottery odds. You can use the provided presets or manually adjust the odds for each team. The total odds must not exceed 1000.

Step 4: Run The Lottery!
Once you're ready, click the "Run The Lottery!" button to see the results. The top 4 picks are revealed manually, just like the real NBA draft lottery.
